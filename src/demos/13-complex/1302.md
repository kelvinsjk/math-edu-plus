# Implemented Questions

## Type 1: 2007 P1 Q3b
  
$$
\begin{equation}
zz^* + az = X + Yi
\end{equation}
$$
where $a, X, Y \in \mathbb{R}$ and $z=x+yi$ where $x,y \in \mathbb{R}$.

Implemented as `type 1` .

`qnCode` for actual question: `12A2`

## Types 2-4

$$
\begin{align}
zz^* + az^* &= X + Yi \\
zz^* + aiz &= X + Yi \\
zz^* + aiz^* &= X + Yi
\end{align}
$$
Implemented as `type 2`, `type 3` and `type 4`
